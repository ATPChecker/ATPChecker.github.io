About ATPChecker

ATPChecker is designed to help developers check whether their developed apps or libraries comply with regulation requirements.

ATPChecker is a tool to analyze the consistency between TPL's privacy policies and user privacy access behaviors, and host app's privacy policies and user privacy interaction with TPLs.
